Exclusively from your own price catalog, which we load from the cost files you already maintain. Quotewright never computes a sale price from cost or margin, and never estimates — every price on a quote is a value from your list, priced in the right currency for each product division. During setup we flag any rows in your file that are missing a price, so your catalog is complete before go-live.

Quotewright reads the entire email thread, not just the newest message. If the first email asked for a product and the third email finally supplied the quantity, it assembles the complete request across all of them and quotes accordingly — the way a good salesperson would.

Quotes are written in the customer's language — Turkish and English today, with more on request. Pricing supports multiple currencies per product line (for example EUR for one division, USD for another) with local-currency equivalents at the central bank's daily rate.
