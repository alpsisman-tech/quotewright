How much runs on its own

The pipeline scores every quote for confidence and margin, then sorts it into a tier. These gates decide where the line sits — and whether the safest quotes can go out without a human tap. The send gate stays off until you turn it on.

Auto-send

The one money-facing switch. Everything else here only sorts quotes into tiers.

Send green-tier quotes automatically
Off — every quote waits for you
When on, only quotes that clear both green gates below send on their own. Amber and red always wait in the console. When off, nothing sends automatically — the pipeline just drafts.
Green · minimum confidence 90/100

How sure the match has to be before a quote counts as green. Higher = the system only trusts itself on near-certain matches.

Green · minimum margin 20%

The deal also has to be healthy. Below this margin a quote can never be green, however confident the match — it drops to amber for a look.

Amber · minimum confidence 60/100

The floor for “worth reviewing.” Above this (but short of green) a quote is amber. Below it, the quote is red — flagged for a careful look.
