2. Scope

This policy covers personal data we collect through the Quotewright website — mainly when you request a pilot or contact us. When Quotewright processes a manufacturer's own catalog, pricing and RFQ correspondence to draft quotes, that is covered by section 5 and a separate data processing agreement.

3. What data we collect

Information you give us

Through our pilot/contact form we collect the details you enter, which may include your name, company, work email, your approximate monthly RFQ volume, and your message. Please don't include sensitive information in free-text fields.

Information collected automatically

Our site is static with no advertising or analytics trackers. Our hosting and form providers process limited technical data (IP address, browser/device type, timestamps) to deliver the site and prevent abuse. Web fonts are served by Google Fonts, which receives your IP address on load (see Cookies).

5. Your catalog & RFQ data

When a manufacturer uses Quotewright, we process their product catalog, pricing files and incoming request-for-quotation emails to draft quotes. In that context the manufacturer is the controller of that data (including any personal data of its customers in RFQ threads) and Quotewright acts as a processor, handling it only on the manufacturer's documented instructions under a data processing agreement. Each client's catalog is stored isolated per client and never shared. Quotewright is draft-first: quotes are prepared for approval and not sent without the client's action. We delete client data on request.

7. International transfers

Some providers are outside the UK/EEA (notably the USA). We rely on an appropriate safeguard — the UK International Data Transfer Agreement/Addendum, EU Standard Contractual Clauses, or an adequacy decision (including the UK/EU–US Data Privacy Framework where the provider participates).

8. How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data as long as needed to deal with your request and for a reasonable period after — typically up to 24 months from last contact — unless the law requires longer. Client catalog and RFQ data are retained per the client agreement and deleted or returned on request.

9. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to processing, data portability, and to withdraw consent. To exercise them, contact us (section 12). You can also complain to a regulator — in the UK the ICO (ico.org.uk); in Türkiye the KVKK Kurumu.

10. Turkey — KVKK

If you're in Türkiye, our processing is also governed by Law No. 6698 (KVKK). See our dedicated KVKK Aydınlatma Metni.

11. Cookies

We don't use advertising or analytics cookies. Details are in our Cookie & Tracking Notice.

12. Contact

For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, email [privacy@seamuk.com] or use our contact form. Our full terms are in the Terms of Use.
