Cookie & Tracking Notice

The short version: our website doesn't track you for advertising or analytics. Here's exactly what it does use.

Last updated: 8 July 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files a website can store on your device; similar technologies (local storage, web fonts) can also read or receive limited information. Under UK PECR and the EU ePrivacy rules, non-essential cookies need your consent; strictly necessary ones don't.

2. What we use

The Quotewright marketing site is deliberately lightweight. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site trackers, or analytics products such as Google Analytics. What may be present:

TechnologyTypePurposeConsent?
Essential local storageFirst-partyBasic UI state (e.g. remembering a dismissed element)No — necessary
Google FontsThird-partyServing typefaces; Google receives your IP on loadData transfer — see below
Netlify / Netlify FormsThird-partyHosting and secure delivery of form submissions; may set an anti-spam tokenNo — necessary

3. A note on Google Fonts

We load fonts from Google's servers, so Google receives your IP address to deliver them. We may move to self-hosting to avoid this; it's disclosed here for transparency.

4. Managing cookies

Because we don't set non-essential cookies, there's nothing to opt out of for tracking. You can still clear or block cookies and site data via your browser settings, though blocking may affect how fonts render.

5. Changes

If we ever add analytics or other non-essential technologies, we'll update this notice and, where required, request your consent through a banner first.

6. More information

See our Privacy Policy and, for Türkiye, our KVKK Aydınlatma Metni. Questions: [privacy@seamuk.com] or the contact form.
