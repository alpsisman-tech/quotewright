How it works

From a raw email to a quote you approve — five steps, minutes apart.

Step 01

Read the thread

It picks the real RFQ out of your inbox and reads the whole conversation — follow-ups merged — in any language.

Step 02

Match your catalogue

Every line is matched to your own products by weight, size and colour — the exact SKU, or the closest with a note.

Step 03

Price cash & term

Both payment columns, box maths, per-customer contract prices, and local-currency totals at today's central-bank rate.

Step 04

Draft the reply

A branded HTML email with a PDF quote attached, written in the customer's own language — ready in your drafts.

Step 05

You approve & send

You read it, tap approve — or edit first. Nothing reaches a customer until a person says so. You are the send button.

The guarantee

It will never invent a price.

Every number on a Quotewright quote traces to a cell in your own price file. No cost-plus, no estimate, no plausible-looking guess. If a line isn't in your catalogue, it's named and handed to your sales team — never filled in.

Priced
Straight from your file

Matched to a real SKU with a real price — cash and term — exactly as your catalogue defines it.

Contract-aware
This customer's agreed price

When a customer has a negotiated price on record, that price wins over the list price — automatically.

Routed
Never guessed

Anything uncataloged is flagged in the quote and passed to your team by name — with no number attached.
