Why factories keep it

Three things a template will never do.

It won't invent a price

Every number traces to a cell in your own price file — never cost-plus, never a guess, never a plausible-looking estimate. If a line isn't in your catalog, it's named and handed to your sales team, not filled in. Auditable by design.

uncataloged line → routed to your team, never faked

It reads every language

A Turkish, German, Bulgarian or English request across three scattered emails becomes one understood order — colour names, units and trade terms translated correctly — and the quote comes back in the customer's own language.

"navy blue" → lacivert · 1.000 Meter → 1,650 m²

Cash & term, side by side

Both payment options priced on every line, with the term days your list defines and local-currency equivalents at today's central-bank rate — and each customer's own contract price applied when one exists.
